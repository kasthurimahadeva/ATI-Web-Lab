world's best scrambles Eggs

This recipe is a very special breakfast :just look at the ingrediants to see why it has to be tasted to be believe

ingrediants

The following ingrediants make one serving

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 table spoon of butter
  • 1/3 cup of cream
  • A pinch of salt
  • Freshly milled black paper
  • 3 fresh chive
  1. Wisk eggs, cream & salt in a bowl
  2. Melt Butter in nonstick pan over a high heat
  3. pour egg mixture into pan and wait until it starts setting around the edge of the pan
  4. using a wooden spatula ,bring the mixture in to the center as if it were an omelet and let it cool for 20 seconds
  5. fold content in again ,leave 20 seconds
  6. Grind a light sprinking of freshly

you should only makes a maximum of 2 servings per frying pan
