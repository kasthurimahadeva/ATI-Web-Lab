World's best scrambles Eggs

This recipe is a very special breakfast: just look at he ingredients to see why it has to be tasted to be believe

Ingredients

The following ingredients make one serving

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 table spoon of butter
  • 1/3 cup of cream
  • A pinch of salt
  • freshly milled black paper
  • 3 fresh chive(chopped)

  1. Wisk aggs, cream & salt in a bowl
  2. Melt Butter in a nonstick pan over a high heat(taking over not to burn the butter)
  3. Pour egg mixture into pan and wait untill it starts setting around the edge of the pan (around 20 seconds
  4. Using a wooden spatula, bring the moxture in to the center as if it were an omelet and let it cool for another 20 seconds
  5. Fold content in again, leave for 20 seconds and repeat until the eggs are only just done
  6. Grind a light sprinkling of freshly milled paper over the eggs and blend in some chopped fresh chives

You should only makes a maximum of 2 serving per frying pan
