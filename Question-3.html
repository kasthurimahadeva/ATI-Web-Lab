World's best scrambles Eggs

This recipe is a very special breakfast :just look at the ingredients to see why it has to be tasted to be believe

Ingredients


The following ingredients make one serving
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 table spoon of butter
  • 1/3 cup of cream
  • A pinch of salt
  • Freshly milled black paper
  • 3 fresh chive(chopped)
  1. Wisk eggs,cream & salt in a bowl
  2. Melt Butter in a nonstick pan over a high heat(taking over not burn be butter)
  3. Pour eggs mixture into pan and wait until it start swtting around the edge of the pan(around 20 second)
  4. Using a woodern spatula,bring the mixture in to the center as if it were an omelet and let it cool for another 20 secound
  5. Fold content in again,leave for 20 second and repeat until the eggs only just done
  6. Grind a light sprinkling of freshly milled paper over the eggs and blend in some chopped fresh chives
You should only makes a maxmimum of 2 servings per frying pan
